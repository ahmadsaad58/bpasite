Marijuana, more commonly known as weed or cannabis, is a plant that contains tetrahydrocannabinol (THC) and several other psychoactive and anxiolytic elements that contribute towards it being the most illicitly used drug in the world. Coming from the cannabis sativa plant (dry hemp plant), weed has grown to be very popular for its ephemerally relaxing and physical effects. Many people continue to use its medicinal purposes as a scapegoat to continuously go on a high or to use the drug addictively. While many people become more and more accustomed towards using weed, they become blinded towards the adverse effects that come with the drug. While these effects may be of minimal importance/possibility in the long run to most people, it is definitive to all people that the use of marijuana has been proven to stress the heart which can lead to a heart attack at any moment. Another major problem that arises is the possibility of synthetic marijuana, which is essentially a deadly concoction of many chemicals of any amount. This has been recognized as a more deadly version of marijuana as it has definitively harmed numerous people who are under the false pretense that is marijuana, when in reality they are drinking none other than their death sentence.
